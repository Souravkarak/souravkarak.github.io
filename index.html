FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is PixelFit really free to use?

Yes — completely free. There are no plans, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees. PixelFit is supported by non-intrusive advertisements.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. PixelFit uses your browser's built-in Canvas API to process images entirely on your device. Your photos never leave your computer — not even for a split second.

What is the maximum image size I can resize?

There's no enforced file-size limit — performance depends on your browser and device memory. Most modern devices handle images up to 50 MB without any issues.

Will resizing reduce image quality?

Enlarging an image beyond its original resolution will reduce sharpness — that's a fundamental limitation of digital photography. Shrinking, however, preserves quality well, especially with JPEG quality set to 85% or higher.

Which format should I use — JPEG, PNG, or WebP?

Use JPEG for photos and social media (small file, wide compatibility). Use PNG when you need a transparent background or pixel-perfect quality for logos. Use WebP for modern websites — it offers the best compression with transparency support.

Can I resize images on mobile?

Absolutely. PixelFit is fully responsive and works on iOS and Android browsers. Just tap "Choose Photo" to access your camera roll.

About PixelFit

PixelFit is a free, browser-based image resizing and compression tool built by Sourav Karak — a web developer who believes that simple, useful tools should be available to everyone, for free.

Our Mission

We believe that basic image editing tools should be free, private, and accessible. PixelFit processes everything locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API — your images are never uploaded to any server. There is no account to create, no watermark added, and no file size limit imposed.

What You Can Do

  • Resize photos by exact pixel dimensions with aspect-ratio lock
  • Scale images by percentage (1% – 200%)
  • Apply one-click social media preset sizes (Instagram, YouTube, WhatsApp, and more)
  • Convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats
  • Smart-compress images to reduce file size automatically
  • Preview changes live before downloading
  • Drag & drop or paste from clipboard — zero friction

Technology

PixelFit is a single-file HTML application with zero external dependencies and no back-end server. Everything runs in your browser using the Canvas 2D API. It is open, lightweight, and fast — even on slow connections.
