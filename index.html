Supported Formats

JPEG, PNG, and WebP explained

Not sure which format to choose? Here's a quick guide to help you pick the right one for your project.

Format Best For Transparency File Size
JPEG Photos, social media posts, blog images Not supported Small — great for fast loading
PNG Logos, screenshots, images with text Supported (alpha) Larger, but lossless quality
WebP Modern websites, Google PageSpeed Supported Smallest — up to 30% smaller than JPEG

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How to resize image online?

Simply select the "Resize Image" tool, drop your photo into the workspace, set your desired width and height (in pixels or percentage), choose your output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP), and click Download. It's fast and processes entirely on your device.

How to convert image to PDF?

Choose the "Image to PDF" tool, upload one or multiple images, drag them to arrange the correct order, select your preferred page size (like A4 or Letter) and margin, and then download your high-quality PDF document instantly.

How to enhance image quality?

Select the "AI Image Enhancer" tool, upload a blurry or low-quality photo, and adjust the enhancement level. You can use features like Smart Denoise, 2× Upscaling, and sharpness controls to improve clarity before downloading the enhanced image.

Is PixelFit free?

Yes — completely free. There are no plans, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees. PixelFit is a free online tool to resize images, convert photos to PDF, and enhance image quality, supported by non-intrusive advertisements.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. PixelFit processes all images locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your computer — not even for a split second.

About PixelFit

PixelFit is a free, browser-based image resizing and compression tool built by Sourav Karak — a web developer who believes that simple, useful tools should be available to everyone, for free.

Our Mission

We believe that basic image editing tools should be free, private, and accessible. PixelFit processes everything locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API — your images are never uploaded to any server. There is no account to create, no watermark added, and no file size limit imposed.

What You Can Do

  • Resize photos by exact pixel dimensions with aspect-ratio lock
  • Scale images by percentage (1% – 200%)
  • Apply one-click social media preset sizes (Instagram, YouTube, WhatsApp, and more)
  • Convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats
  • Smart-compress images to reduce file size automatically
  • Preview changes live before downloading
  • Drag & drop or paste from clipboard — zero friction

Technology

PixelFit is a single-file HTML application with zero external dependencies and no back-end server. Everything runs in your browser using the Canvas 2D API. It is open, lightweight, and fast — even on slow connections.
